FAQ

Common questions

How do I pay?
We accept CliQ transfers and cash-on-delivery for Amman and Zarqa. No international cards required. Payment instructions are shown at checkout.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel before your next billing date and you will not be charged again. You keep access until the end of the period you already paid for.
Are the questions actually from the real Nazari test?
Our question bank is compiled from students who recently took and passed the test. It is not an official government document, but it reflects the real question pool as closely as possible. We update it regularly as new questions are reported.
What language is the test in?
The official Nazari test is in Arabic. NazariPro supports both Arabic and English so you can study in either language, but we recommend practicing in Arabic to match the real exam environment.
What if I still fail after using Premium?
Contact us and we will give you a free additional month of Premium access and connect you with one of our tutors. Your success is the product.
